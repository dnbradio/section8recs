Section 8 Recordings

About Us

Established in 2007, Section 8 Recordings is a pioneering record label dedicated to the darker realms of electronic music. Specializing in genres such as Darkstep, Hardstep, Technoid, Dark Dubstep, and Glitch, we are committed to unearthing and promoting innovative artists who push the boundaries of sound.

Our mission is to provide a platform for both emerging and established artists to showcase their unique styles, contributing to the evolution of the electronic music scene. Over the years, we have built a diverse catalog that resonates with listeners seeking deep, immersive, and cutting-edge auditory experiences.

Our Music

At Section 8 Recordings, we pride ourselves on a rich and varied discography. Our releases feature a blend of dark, deep, and heavy tracks that have captivated audiences worldwide. Notable releases include:

  • "The Slayer" by Accelerated Culture (2012)
  • "Asylum EP" by E-Sassin (2018)
  • "The Hack" by Sick Cycle (2019)
